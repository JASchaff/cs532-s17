Citibank: Stop funding the Dakota Access Pipeline today!

Tell Citibank to stop funding human rights abuses and environmental destruction by ending its support of the Dakota Access Pipeline today!

Citibank is the lead funder of the Dakota Access Pipeline – a project in the United States that tramples on the rights of Indigenous Peoples and has led to rampant police violence against unarmed water protectors.

Donald Trump and the Army Corps of Engineers just decided to disregard the pipeline's environmental review process and let construction proceed. We can still stop the pipeline if institutions like Citibank pull funding and give the developers no choice but to back out.
