Twitter Reacts to Jill Stein’s Nonsensical Tweet Blaming Democrats for DeVos Confirmation

by Justin Baragona | 3:57 pm, February 7th, 2017
 

For those paying attention to the news today, you probably noticed that President Donald Trump’s Secretary of Education nominee, Betsy DeVos, was confirmed by the Senate today. The final vote was 51-50 as Vice President Mike Pence had to cast a historical tie-breaking vote due to the fact that all 48 Senators in the Democratic Caucus, along with two Republicans, voted against the confirmation.

The vote today followed a 24-hour hold of the Senate floor by Democrats in which they hoped they’d be able to convince at least one more Republican to switch sides and go against DeVos, knowing that a tie would mean Pence would have to step in.

Well, earlier today, Green Party Presidential candidate Jill Stein took to Twitter to blame DeVos’s successful appointment on…Democrats?!?

Why would we have a tie on such an egregious nominee? Because Democrats serve corporate interests. https://t.co/66rpL1ifik

— Dr. Jill Stein (@DrJillStein) February 7, 2017
