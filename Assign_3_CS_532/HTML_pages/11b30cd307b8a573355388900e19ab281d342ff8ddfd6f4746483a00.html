Lady Gaga Is on Fire, Will Appear on Season 9 of RuPaul's Drag Race

X
Shop This Post
share
February 7, 2017 @ 2:15 PM
by: Jonathan Borge

Gentleman, start your engines, and may the best woman, win!

That’s the catchphrase Emmy Award-winning drag queen and TV host RuPaul uses season after season on RuPaul’s Drag Race. And that’s exactly what Lady Gaga is set to soon repeat.

Yes, Mother Monster has finally agreed to appear as a guest judge on Logo’s hit comedic show and the preview proves that the songstress contains charisma, uniqueness, nerve, and talent—the four traits RuPaul looks for in “America’s Next Drag Superstar.” On Tuesday, the team behind the show cleverly made the announcement by responding to one of Gaga’s tweets from 2013.

Back then, she wrote, “Can I PLEASE be a judge on drag race! @RuPaul I started out in these club, these women taught me how to serve!” Four years later, RuPaul’s team replied and wrote, “Wish finally granted Mother Monster.”

In the preview clip below, Gaga basically makes every gay boy’s ultimate dream come true and stands next to RuPaul dressed in a look that gives Season 9’s contestants a serious run for their money.
