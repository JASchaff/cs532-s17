Caught In A “Bad RuMance”: Lady Gaga Is Coming To “RuPaul’s Drag Race” Season 9

And the queens CANNOT handle it.
by Christopher Rudolph 2/7/2017

After her epic Super Bowl Halftime Show, and the announcement of her Joanne world tour, Lady Gaga is now sashaying into the workroom!

That’s right, Mother Monster is coming to RuPaul’s Drag Race as the special guest judge on the Season 9 premiere! What a way to kick things off!

Gaga tweeted out the announcement today which included a clip of Ru introducing the “Applause” diva to the Season 9 queens. Watch their reaction as they turn into a screaming pile of wigs and heels—can you blame them?

“The season nine premiere event features a shocking opening that will go down as one of the most jaw-dropping moments in Drag Race history,” Logo said in a statement about Gaga’s RuVeal.

Little Monsters, how are we feeling about this fabulous news?
